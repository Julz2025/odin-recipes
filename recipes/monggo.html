Sauteed mung beans

Another dish I learned to cook and love whilst at university, which has since continued to be mine and my partner's comfort food.

Ingredients:

  • Mung beans
  • Spinach
  • Fish sauce
  • Sliced cooked smoked mackerel
  • Crushed garlic cloves
  • Diced onion
  • Diced ginger
  • Diced tomato
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable cube

Steps:

  1. Thoroughly wash the mung beans.
  2. Place the mung beans into a deep pan and pour water until all beans are submerged.
  3. Let the mung beans come to a boil. Once boiling, turn down the heat to simmer until beans are soft.
  4. Whilst the beans are simmering, prepare the aromatics.
  5. Heat up oil in a pan and sautee the onions along with tomatoes and ginger.
  6. Add garlic and continue to sautee for a few minutes.
  7. Add the mung beans to the pan and let it simmer with the aromatics. Season with salt, pepper and fish sauce.
  8. Add some more water if you prefer the dish to be saucier.
  9. Crumble the vegetable cube and let it come to a gentle boil.
  10. Sprinkle the sliced cooked smoked mackerel and add the spinach leaves.
  11. Turn down the heat and continue to simmer until the spinach has wilted.
  12. Serve hot with white steamed rice. Enjoy!
